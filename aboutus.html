Introduction:
The Liberty County Sheriff's Office (LCSO) is one of the most active departments in the Springfield RP community. Our main purpose is to uphold the laws of Springfield and keep the community safe and secure. Our team of over 90 dedicated sheriffs works hard to protect Springfield, responding to calls, assisting the civilians, and maintaining peace in all areas of the Springfield.

The LCSO is organized into different divisions, each with specific responsibilities, so we can cover a wide range of situations effectively. These divisions allow our sheriffs to focus on specific areas of law enforcement. Each division plays a key role in our overall mission, ensuring that we’re always ready to meet the needs of the community.

We also have a structured rank system within the department. This rank system allows sheriffs to start in entry-level positions and work their way up to higher ranks. Moving up in rank provides opportunities to take on more responsibility and develop important skills. Each rank allows sheriffs to gain experience and knowledge so they can take on leadership roles within the department.
