About Us

The Liberty County Sheriff's Office (LCSO) is one of the most active departments in the Springfield RP community. Our main purpose is to uphold the laws of Springfield and keep the community safe and secure. Our team of over 90 dedicated sheriffs works hard to protect Springfield, responding to calls, assisting the civilians, and maintaining peace in all areas of the Springfield.
